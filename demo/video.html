Core Innovation

W3C VC Delegation — Not Self-Grant

Counterparty wallet authenticates as separate T3N identity. Signs delegation credential with EIP-191. Agent can ONLY act within the scope defined. T3N runtime enforces — without grant: 403 Forbidden.
